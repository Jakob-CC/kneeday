Save the date: September 25-28, 2024

We invite you to join the first on site international knee day!

What a great opportunity to present your scientific work embedded in the surrounding of international highlight speakers from all over the world. The concept of the first international knee days providing a mix of update lectures, battles, live surgical demonstrations, video demos, workshops and free papers!
This is a huge possibility for you to present your scientific work, prove your methods and strategies during the discussion and doublecheck the strengths and flaws of your paper before sending it for publication. We are looking forward to your exciting abstracts!

Pre-courses

The congress kick off starts with 2 pre-courses: Osteotomy course and basic arthroscopy course. The osteotomy course takes place at the Martin Luther hospital and the basic arthroscopy course will be held at the Karl Storz house in the middle of Berlin.
